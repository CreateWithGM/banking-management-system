Term Deposit Example

Key Features of Term Deposits

  • Fixed Interest Rate: The interest rate is fixed and does not change.
  • Specified Term: Money is deposited for a specific period.
  • Higher Interest Rates: Term deposits offer higher rates than savings accounts.
  • Penalty for Early Withdrawal: Withdrawing before term end incurs a penalty.
  • Safe Investment: Backed by banks, offering guaranteed returns.

Example: Term Deposit of ₹1000

  • Principal Amount: ₹1000
  • Interest Rate: 6% per annum
  • Term Duration: 1 year

Interest earned in a year: ₹60

Maturity Amount: ₹1060

Benefits of Term Deposits

  • Guaranteed Returns
  • Safe Investment
  • Higher Interest Rates

Considerations

  • Liquidity: Funds are locked for the term.
  • Inflation: Fixed rate may not keep up with inflation.

Summary

  • Term Deposit Amount: ₹1000
  • Interest Rate: 6% per annum
  • Term Duration: 1 year
  • Interest Earned: ₹60
  • Maturity Amount: ₹1060

Term deposits offer a safe investment option with guaranteed returns over a fixed period, suitable for stable investment needs.
