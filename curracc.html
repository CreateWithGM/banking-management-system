Current Account Example

John's Current Account

  • Bank: XYZ Bank
  • Minimum Balance: $5,000
  • Features:
    • No interest earned on the balance
    • Unlimited transactions
    • Overdraft facility available
    • Access to online and mobile banking
    • Checkbook issued for payments

John deposits $10,000 into his current account. He uses this account to pay his suppliers, receive payments from customers, and manage other business expenses. The overdraft facility provides him with flexibility in case he needs additional funds for short-term needs.

Raj's Current Account

  • Initial Deposit: ₹1000
  • Interest Rate: 0% (no interest earned)
  • Minimum Balance Requirement: ₹5000
  • Transaction Limit: Unlimited transactions
  • Usage:
    • Raj uses his current account for daily business transactions.
    • He deposits ₹1000 to start but needs to maintain a minimum balance of ₹5000 to avoid penalties.
    • Since the current account does not earn interest, the ₹1000 balance remains ₹1000 unless he makes withdrawals or additional deposits.
