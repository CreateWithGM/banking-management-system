Savings Round Up Feature

When you make an online purchase or transaction, the amount is rounded up to the nearest whole number.

The rounded-up amount is transferred from your online wallet or account to your savings account.

Over time, these small amounts add up, and you'll have a nice sum of money saved in your savings account.

Example:

Transaction Amount Rounded Up Amount Savings
Online food order ₹52.75 ₹53.00 ₹0.25
Online shopping ₹148.25 ₹149.00 ₹0.75
Online bill payment ₹27.50 ₹28.00 ₹0.50
Total Savings ₹1.50

By the end of the day, you would have saved a total of ₹1.50 in your savings account.

This feature encourages people to save money without feeling the pinch, as the amounts are small and seemingly insignificant.

It's a great way to build an emergency fund or save for specific goals, like a vacation or a big purchase.
