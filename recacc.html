Recurring Deposit Example

Key Features of Recurring Deposits

  • Regular Deposits: Fixed amount deposited regularly.
  • Fixed Interest Rate: Interest rate remains constant.
  • Specified Term: Deposits made for a specific period.
  • Interest Calculation: Interest calculated on total deposits.
  • Safe Investment: Backed by banks, offering guaranteed returns.

Example: Recurring Deposit with ₹1000

  • Monthly Deposit Amount: ₹1000
  • Interest Rate: 5% per annum
  • Term Duration: 1 year

Total Deposits in a Year: ₹12000

Interest earned in a year: ₹600

Maturity Amount: ₹12600

Benefits of Recurring Deposits

  • Regular Savings: Helps in disciplined savings.
  • Fixed Returns: Guaranteed returns with fixed interest rate.
  • Flexible Terms: Various term options available.

Considerations

  • Penalties: Missing deposits may lead to penalties.
  • Fixed Deposits: Less flexible compared to other investments.

Summary

  • Monthly Deposit Amount: ₹1000
  • Interest Rate: 5% per annum
  • Term Duration: 1 year
  • Total Deposits in a Year: ₹12000
  • Interest Earned: ₹600
  • Maturity Amount: ₹12600

Recurring deposits provide a systematic way to save and earn returns, suitable for individuals looking for regular savings with guaranteed returns.
