Savings Account Example

Riya's Savings Account

  • Initial Deposit: ₹1000
  • Interest Rate: 4% per annum
  • Minimum Balance Requirement: ₹500
  • Transaction Limit: 10 free transactions per month

Calculation of Interest:

Interest = Principal × Rate × Time

Interest = ₹1000 × 4/100 × 1 = ₹40

At the end of the year, Riya's balance would be: ₹1000 + ₹40 = ₹1040
